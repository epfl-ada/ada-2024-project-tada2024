Northwest Africa or Northwestern Africa is a variably defined  region of the African continent. The term is commonly used in various disciplines: geopolitics, archaeology, anthropology, and genetics. Incorporating  cardinal directions, the term is preferred over use of Maghreb and similar terms possibly due to the cultural underpinnings of those terms.The countries and territories in Northwestern Africa include:
AlgeriaLibyaMoroccoMauritaniaTunisiaWestern Sahara
Maghreb,  Barbary, and  Tamazgha are more or less synonymous with Northwestern Africa.
