In anatomy, the orbit is the cavity or socket of the  skull in which the eye and its  appendages are situated.It can also mean the skin which surrounds the eye of a bird.

 Bones
In humans, seven bones make up the bony orbit:
 Frontal bone Zygomatic bone Maxillary bone Sphenoid bone Ethmoid bone Palatine bone Lacrimal bone
 Foramina and openings
